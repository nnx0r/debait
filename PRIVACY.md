# DeBait — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 22 June 2026_

**DeBait does not collect, store, transmit, share, or sell any personal data or
user data of any kind.**

The extension runs entirely on your device, inside your browser:

- It replaces clickbait YouTube thumbnails with the video's real auto-generated
  first frame by redirecting thumbnail image requests with a static
  `declarativeNetRequest` ruleset. This redirect happens locally in the browser.
- No browsing history, URLs, search queries, images, or any other information is
  read for analytics, logged, or sent to us or to any third party.
- There are no analytics, no trackers, no cookies, no accounts, and no remote
  servers operated by DeBait. The extension makes no network requests of its own.
- The only network activity is your browser loading YouTube thumbnail images as
  it already does — DeBait merely points those requests at the real video frame.

## Permissions

DeBait requests access only to `youtube.com` and `ytimg.com`, solely to replace
thumbnails on YouTube. It does not access any other website.
